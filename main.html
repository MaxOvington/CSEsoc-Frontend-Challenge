A welcome to all Simians

Human, gorilla, gibbon, chimp.

We are all bound by the same intrinsic drive for curiosity and innovation - a shared legacy of great innovations, and ingenuity despite the odds.

I believe in the pursuit great engineering and software design much can be learned from our primate brethren.

Time and time again various apes and monkeys have shown the capacity for creativity, lateral thinking and collaboration in miriad of environments and circumstances.

As humans we may have come a long way from our arboreal, tree dwelling origins. Yet in tackling the technical problems of tomorrow I believe we still have much to gain from the widsom of the monke.
